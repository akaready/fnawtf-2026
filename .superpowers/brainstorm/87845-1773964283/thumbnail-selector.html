Behaviors

Hover over video

Video starts playing from the saved thumbnail_time position (same as proposal page hover preview). Shows what clients will see.

Mouse leaves video

Snaps back to the current thumbnail frame. Pauses video. You always see the saved thumbnail when not hovering.

Drag the slider

A 2px white vertical line. Drag left/right to scrub the video timeline. Video frame updates in real-time as you drag. The purple marker shows where the current thumbnail is.

Click / Release drag

Sets the thumbnail at that frame. Captures hi-res frame, uploads to Supabase, updates thumbnail_url + thumbnail_time. Purple marker moves to the new position.
